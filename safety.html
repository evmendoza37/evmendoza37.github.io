Safety Tips

Hopefully, you live somewhere you don't have to feel worried walking down the street. However, if you do, here are some helpful general tips to protecting yourself.

1. While walking alone on the street, especially at night, do not wear earbuds or headphones, and be aware of your surroundings.

2. If necessary, yelling "Fire!" generally attracts more people than yelling "Help!"

3. Be careful of where and for what reason you share sensitive information.
